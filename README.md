# 🎬 MediaTrack

> **Every Story Leaves a Mark.**

MediaTrack is a modern web application designed for people who love stories. Whether it's an anime, a TV series, or a movie, MediaTrack helps users keep track of their entertainment journey in one place.

Instead of simply marking titles as watched, MediaTrack allows users to follow their viewing progress, organize personal watchlists, maintain favorite collections, and build a profile that reflects their unique taste in entertainment.

The goal is to create a clean, cinematic, and intuitive platform where every story you've experienced becomes part of your personal journey.

---

## ✨ Features

* 🎬 Track Movies, TV Series & Anime
* 📺 Continue Watching Progress
* ⭐ Personal Favorites
* 📝 Ratings & Reviews
* 📚 Custom Watchlists
* 📊 Viewing Statistics
* 👤 Personalized User Profiles
* 🔍 Media Search
* 🎞️ Beautiful Cinematic Interface

## 💡 Inspiration

MediaTrack is inspired by the idea that every movie, series, and anime we watch becomes part of our journey. Rather than focusing only on ratings, the platform is designed to help users remember where they left off, organize their favorite stories, and build a personal media archive that grows over time.
